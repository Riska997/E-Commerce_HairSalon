About Us

Good Hair style Good Selfie

Salon Eve & Steve was established in 1997 at Westgate Mall Mitchell's Plain from very humble beginnings. We are a hair salon with a modern style and a hip and funky upbeat. We treat our clients with a lot of respect, and all clients will get quality and individual attention.
